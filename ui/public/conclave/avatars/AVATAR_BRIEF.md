# Brief: 8 collectible avatars for "Conclave" AI agents

You are generating a **cohesive set of 8 character avatars** for a collectible web game called **Conclave**: autonomous AI agents that scheme, ally, and betray each other for a pot, in the style of *The Traitors*. Players own, collect, and breed these agents, so each avatar must read as a **premium, ownable collectible** (think a polished trading-card / NFT character set), and the 8 must look like **one matching collection**, not 8 unrelated images.

## Deliverables (exact)

- **8 PNG files**, one per character, saved to: `ui/public/conclave/avatars/`
- **Filenames (exact, lowercase):** `schemer.png`, `charmer.png`, `inquisitor.png`, `zealot.png`, `brute.png`, `trickster.png`, `martyr.png`, `ghost.png`
- **Size:** 1024×1024, square. Centered bust/portrait with a little headroom.
- **Background:** dark and moody (near-black, faint candlelit vignette) OR transparent — pick one and use it for **all 8** identically, so they tile as a set. The app composites them on a dark UI.
- **No text, no names, no frames, no logos** in the image. The UI adds names and card frames.

## Shared art direction (apply to all 8 identically)

- **Concept:** masked conspirators of a secret society / hooded figures at a candlelit round table. Mysterious, dramatic, a little sinister, but beautiful and high-status.
- **Style:** stylized painterly or stylized-3D character art, premium and clean. Consistent rendering, lighting, and level of detail across all 8. One dramatic key light (candle/low warm source) plus the character's own colored rim-glow.
- **Framing & scale:** identical for all 8 — head-and-shoulders, facing forward (slight turn ok), same crop and proportions, so they line up in a grid like a deck of cards.
- **Color discipline:** each character is dominated by **its own signature color** (listed below) as rim light / glow / accents, over the shared dark base. This color-coding is load-bearing (the game uses it), so make it unmistakable.
- **Cohesion test:** placed side by side, they must obviously be the same set, same world, same hand.

## The 8 characters

Each is a hidden-role social player; the face is a stylized **mask**. Match the expression and motif to the personality.

1. **schemer** — signature **violet `#A78BFA`**. The cold mastermind: builds trust only to spend it, times the betrayal. Sleek, sharp, aristocratic mask; half-lidded calculating eyes; a faint knowing smirk. Motif: chess / coiled serpent / interlocking rings. Elegant menace.
2. **charmer** — signature **rose-pink `#F472B6`**. Everyone's friend, the velvet manipulator you'd never suspect. Beautiful, warm, inviting; soft open eyes; a genuine-seeming smile. Motif: a rose. Magnetic and disarming.
3. **inquisitor** — signature **steel-blue `#60A5FA`**. The relentless prosecutor who reads the room out loud. Severe, intense interrogator; piercing focused eyes (a lens/monocle motif works); stern flat mouth. Motif: an eye / scales of justice. Cold scrutiny.
4. **zealot** — signature **amber-gold `#FBBF24`**. The true-believer crusader; loyalty as religion. Fervent and radiant; a crown of flame; burning exalted eyes; righteous expression. Motif: flame / sunburst halo. Fanatical glow.
5. **brute** — signature **rust-orange `#FB923C`**. Blunt force; bulldozes the vote. Heavy, massive, cracked-stone-or-iron mask; small hard eyes; a clenched heavy jaw; a scar or crack. Motif: a fist / anvil. Grounded and dangerous.
6. **trickster** — signature **cyan `#22D3EE`**. Chaos agent, unreadable, lies for the fun of it. Asymmetric jester mask; mismatched eyes; a crooked grin; harlequin diamond patterns. Motif: dice / harlequin diamond. Playful, off-kilter menace.
7. **martyr** — signature **emerald `#34D399`**. Weaponizes sympathy; the noble victim who is secretly the sharpest player. Serene, sorrowful; a faint halo; downcast glistening eyes; a gentle resigned mouth; a single tear. Motif: halo / teardrop. Saintly, with one hidden glint of cunning.
8. **ghost** — signature **slate-grey `#94A3B8`**. The invisible survivor; says nothing, somehow always still in the room. Faint and semi-translucent, fading/dissolving at the edges; hollow blank eyes; barely-there features; wisps of smoke. Motif: a vanishing wisp. Eerie, forgettable on purpose (lower contrast than the others, on purpose).

## Reminders

- Strong, unmistakable signature color per character.
- No text or frames baked in.
- Deliver all 8 PNGs named exactly as above in `ui/public/conclave/avatars/`.
